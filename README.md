##  Project Overview

This project analyzes OLA ride booking data using **SQL** and **Power BI** to uncover valuable business insights related to ride demand, cancellations, customer satisfaction, revenue, payment methods, and vehicle performance.

The project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis skills, including data cleaning, SQL querying, KPI creation, dashboard development, and business insight generation.

#  SQL Business Problems Solved

- Retrieve all successful bookings
- Calculate average ride distance by vehicle type
- Find total cancelled rides by customers
- Identify top 5 customers by booking frequency
- Count driver cancellations due to personal/car issues
- Find highest and lowest driver ratings
- Retrieve UPI payments
- Calculate average customer rating by vehicle
- Calculate total successful booking value
- List incomplete rides with reasons

#  Key Insights

- Prime Sedan generated the highest booking revenue.
- UPI emerged as the most preferred payment method.
- Most ride cancellations were initiated by customers.
- Weekend ride demand was significantly higher than weekdays.
- Average customer rating remained above 4.2 across most vehicle categories.
- Premium vehicle types recorded higher average booking values.

---

#  Business Recommendations

- Improve driver availability during peak hours.
- Introduce incentives to reduce driver cancellations.
- Offer promotional discounts for underutilized vehicle categories.
- Increase cashback offers on digital payments.
- Improve ETA accuracy to reduce customer cancellations.
